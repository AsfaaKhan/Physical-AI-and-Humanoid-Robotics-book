# Resources

This appendix provides additional resources for further learning and reference in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

## Online Resources

### ROS and ROS2
- [ROS.org](https://www.ros.org/) - Official ROS website with documentation and tutorials
- [ROS Index](https://index.ros.org/) - Package index for ROS ecosystem
- [ROS Discourse](https://discourse.ros.org/) - Community discussion forum
- [ROS Answers](https://answers.ros.org/questions/) - Q&A platform for ROS questions

### Simulation Platforms
- [Gazebo](http://gazebosim.org/) - Official Gazebo simulation website
- [Isaac Sim](https://developer.nvidia.com/isaac-sim) - NVIDIA Isaac Sim documentation
- [Unity Robotics Hub](https://github.com/Unity-Technologies/Unity-Robotics-Hub) - GitHub repository for Unity robotics tools
- [Webots](https://cyberbotics.com/) - Web-based robot simulator

### AI and Machine Learning
- [PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/) - Deep learning framework
- [TensorFlow](https://www.tensorflow.org/) - Machine learning platform
- [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/) - Platform for transformer models and datasets
- [OpenAI](https://openai.com/) - Research organization and API provider

## Books and Textbooks

### Core Robotics
- Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (Eds.). (2016). *Springer Handbook of Robotics*. Springer.
- Spong, M. W., Hutchinson, S., & Vidyasagar, M. (2006). *Robot Modeling and Control*. Wiley.
- Murray, R. M., Li, Z., & Sastry, S. S. (1994). *A Mathematical Introduction to Robotic Manipulation*. CRC Press.

### Humanoid Robotics
- Kajita, S. (2020). *Introduction to Humanoid Robotics*. Springer.
- Ogata, N. (2019). *Humanoid Robotics: A Reference*. Springer.
- Cheng, D. (2007). *Geometric Programming for Design and Cost Minimization*. Society of Automotive Engineers.

### AI and Machine Learning for Robotics
- Goodfellow, I., Bengio, Y., & Courville, A. (2016). *Deep Learning*. MIT Press.
- Russell, S., & Norvig, P. (2020). *Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach* (4th ed.). Pearson.
- Sutton, R. S., & Barto, A. G. (2018). *Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction* (2nd ed.). MIT Press.

## Standards and Safety

### Robotics Standards
- [ISO 13482](https://www.iso.org/standard/52907.html) - Safety requirements for personal care robots
- [ISO 18646](https://www.iso.org/standard/66471.html) - Performance metrics for service robots
- [IEEE Standards for Robotics](https://standards.ieee.org/industry-connections/icsg/robotics/) - Various robotics standards

### Safety Guidelines
- [ISO 10218:2011](https://www.iso.org/standard/45566.html) - Safety requirements for industrial robots
- [ISO/TS 15066](https://www.iso.org/standard/63944.html) - Collaborative robots safety guidelines
